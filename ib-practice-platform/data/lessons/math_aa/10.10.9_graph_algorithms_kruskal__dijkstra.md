Title: Graph Algorithms (Kruskal & Dijkstra)

Introduction: Welcome to our journey through graph algorithms! This unit will explore a fascinating concept that has far-reaching applications in computer science and data structures. We'll delve into the world of graph algorithms, which are based on mathematical principles that govern how information is organized and manipulated within graphs.

Objective: To understand the basic idea behind Kruskal's and Dijkstra's algorithm for traversing a graph efficiently without loops or cycles.

In this lesson, we'll dive into the fascinating realm of graph algorithms and discover why they are so powerful tools in computer science. We'll explore both fundamental concepts and advanced techniques to see how these algorithms can help us traverse graphs more elegantly, even when dealing with complex networks. From basic operations like finding the shortest path (Kruskal's algorithm) to sophisticated methods like dynamic programming, we'll show that graph algorithms are not only efficient but also remarkably elegant in their design.
