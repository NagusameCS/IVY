### Question: What is thermodynamics and why is it important?

**Topic:** Thermodynamics

**Learning Objective:**
Thermodynamics is a fundamental branch of physics that deals with the behavior of matter in relation to temperature. It involves understanding the relationships between energy transfer, heat transfer, work, and energy change.

"The study of the interactions and processes which define the thermodynamic properties of substances." (B.B.4 - The Particulate Nature of Matter)
