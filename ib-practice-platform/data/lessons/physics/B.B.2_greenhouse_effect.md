**Title:** The Greenhouse Effect
## Key Concepts:
1. **Physics:** Solar radiation and heat transfer
2. **Unit of Measure:** Watts or Joules
3. **Unit of Conversion:** Kelvin to Rankine Celsius to Fahrenheit or Celsius to Fahrenheit

4. **Background Information:** The Earth's atmosphere is warmed by the Sun's radiation, primarily emitted in the form of infrared radiation. This warming effect occurs at a rate that depends on the temperature difference between the ground and the top of the atmosphere (the tropopause).
5. **Example: A Greenhouse:** A greenhouse is used to trap heat from the Sun which warms the Earth, allowing plants to grow and animals to live.
