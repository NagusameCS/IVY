**Title:** Doppler Effect

**Description:**
This lesson aims to explain how changes in wavelength of sound waves caused by wind or other external factors can affect an object's motion. The focus will be on the Doppler effect, which is a fundamental concept that governs our everyday experience and scientific understanding.
