## Summary

- The ideal gas equation  = nRT$ relates pressure, volume, temperature, and amount
- Individual gas laws hold specific variables constant
- Kinetic energy of gas molecules is proportional to absolute temperature
- Real gases deviate from ideal behaviour at high pressures and low temperatures
