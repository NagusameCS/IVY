## 1. Taylor Polynomial
- P_n(x)=Σ_{k=0}^n f^{(k)}(a)/k! (x-a)^k
- Remainder R_{n+1} = f^{(n+1)}(c)/(n+1)! (x-a)^{n+1}

## 2. Maclaurin Special Series
- e^x=Σ x^n/n!
- sin x=Σ (-1)^n x^{2n+1}/(2n+1)!
- cos x=Σ (-1)^n x^{2n}/(2n)!
- ln(1+x)=Σ (-1)^{n+1} x^n / n (n≥1, |x|<1)
- (1+x)^p=Σ (p choose n) x^n
