# 1.5 Complex Numbers (Basics)

## Objectives
- Introduce i = √(-1)
- Represent complex numbers in Cartesian form
- Perform arithmetic (add, subtract, multiply, divide)

## 1. Definitions
- z = a + ib, a = Re(z), b = Im(z)
- Conjugate: \overline{z} = a - ib
- Modulus: |z| = √(a^2 + b^2)
- Argument: θ = arg(z)

## 2. Operations
- Addition/Subtraction: combine components
- Multiplication: (a+ib)(c+id) = (ac - bd) + i(ad+bc)
- Division: multiply numerator & denominator by conjugate

## 3. Properties
- z \overline{z} = |z|^2
- Re(z) = (z+\overline{z})/2
- Im(z) = (z-\overline{z})/(2i)

## 4. Practice Prompts
1. Compute modulus & argument
2. Simplify complex expression
3. Write reciprocal 1/z in a+ib form
