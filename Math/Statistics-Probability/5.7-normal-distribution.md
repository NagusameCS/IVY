# 5.7 Normal Distribution

## Objectives
- Understand properties of normal distribution
- Standardization

## 1. Properties
- Symmetric, bell-shaped, mean=median=mode
- 68-95-99.7 empirical rule

## 2. Standardization
- Z = (X-μ)/σ ~ N(0,1)
