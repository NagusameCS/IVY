# 5.4 Conditional Probability & Bayes

## Objectives
- Apply conditional probability definition
- Identify independence
- Use Bayes' theorem (≤3 events)

## 1. Conditional
- P(A|B)=P(A∩B)/P(B)

## 2. Independence
- P(A|B)=P(A) ⇒ events independent

## 3. Bayes
- P(A|B)=P(B|A)P(A)/P(B)
