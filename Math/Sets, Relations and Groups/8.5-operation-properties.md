# 8.5 Operation Properties

## Objectives
- Associative, distributive, commutative properties

## 1. Definitions
- Associative: (a*b)*c = a*(b*c)
- Commutative: a*b = b*a
- Distributive: a*(b∘c)=a*b ∘ a*c
