IVYSTUDY is a free, open-source IB resource platform built by students, for students — and designed to work alongside your teaching, not replace it.

What is IVYSTUDY?

How Educators Use IVYSTUDY

1

Supplement Classroom Lessons

2

Use deep links to share specific lessons directly with students via Google Classroom, email, or your LMS. Students land right on the content — no searching needed.

Use our Exam Timer for timed practice sessions in class. Presets for every IB paper with per-question pacing.

Direct students to our IA/EE Exemplars library to see real examples of successful work, scored and categorized by subject and grade.

5

Contribute Content

IVYSTUDY is open-source. Educators can contribute guides, suggest corrections, or help review content through our GitHub repository.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is IVYSTUDY free to use? expand_more

Yes, completely free. IVYSTUDY is an open-source project. There are no ads, no subscriptions, and no paywalls. All content is licensed under Creative Commons BY-NC-SA 4.0.

Is the content aligned to the IB curriculum? expand_more

Content is written by IB students and organized by syllabus topic. While we strive for accuracy, we recommend using IVYSTUDY as a supplement alongside official IB resources and textbooks.

Can I share content with my students? expand_more

Absolutely! You can share direct links to any lesson. Content is designed to be shared — the Creative Commons license allows it for non-commercial educational use.

How can I contribute or report errors? expand_more

Visit our GitHub repository to submit issues, suggest edits, or contribute new content. We welcome educator input to improve accuracy and coverage.

Does IVYSTUDY replace textbooks? expand_more

No. IVYSTUDY is designed as a supplementary resource — concise, accessible study guides that complement textbooks and classroom teaching. We encourage students to use multiple resources.

Is student data collected? expand_more

IVYSTUDY collects minimal data — just an anonymous page view counter. We don't track individual users, collect personal information, or use cookies for advertising. Privacy is a priority.
